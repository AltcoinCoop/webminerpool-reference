Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start?

Enter a Monero/Aeon address and select a pool. Click "Start mining".

Where do I get a Monero/Aeon address?

You can easily create a new address at moneroaddress.org.

How does it work?

Your browser is mining Monero/Aeon. It has to solve "jobs". Solved jobs are transmitted to the selected pool. Visit the pool website to see how much you have earned.

Something is not working!

Update your browser and try again. If the problem persists contact us.

How much of my hashrate do you keep?

3 %. With more users the fee will get lower.

Can you add my pool?

Yes. It has to be active for some time and have a certain hash rate. Please contact us.
